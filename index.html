What is VeloFlow?

VeloFlow is a powerful desktop application that allows you to monitor and manage your internet usage. Whether you're a casual user or a professional, VeloFlow provides you with the tools and insights to track and optimize your internet consumption.

  • Keep a close eye on your internet usage in real-time.
  • VeloFlow provides real-time monitoring of your internet speeds, allowing you to view your current download and upload speeds at a glance.
  • VeloFlow is designed to be unobtrusive and lightweight, consuming minimal system resources. It runs silently in the background and provides instant speed updates.
